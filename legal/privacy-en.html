2. What we process

  • Account: Google profile (sub, email, name, picture) via OAuth.
  • Trainer data: hands played, decisions, EV estimates, cards in simulation.
  • Imported sessions: parsed hand history and GTO analysis (uploaded .txt).
  • Raw session files: stored only on your device (not synced to cloud).
  • AI Coach (optional): hand/session poker data you explicitly send after consent.

3. Purposes and legal bases (GDPR)

  • Provide the service: contract performance.
  • AI Coach: consent.
  • Non-essential analytics (if enabled): consent.

4. Processors

Google (login & AI), Supabase (database/sync), payment processor when subscriptions are live, GitHub Pages (hosting).

5. Your rights

Access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction, objection. Use Export my data and Delete my account in the account menu, or email the controller.

6. Retention

While your account is active; deleted on account erasure (cloud + local on confirmation).
